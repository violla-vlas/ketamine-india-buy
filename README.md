## What is Ketamine and Its Uses in India?
Ketamine is a dissociative anesthetic medication primarily used in medical settings for sedation, pain management, and treating depression. In India, it's approved for veterinary and human use under strict regulations. As of 2026, its application has expanded in mental health treatments, particularly for treatment-resistant depression via ketamine infusion therapy.

India's pharmaceutical industry produces high-quality ketamine generics, making it accessible through legitimate channels. However, due to its potential for misuse, it's classified as a Schedule X drug, requiring a prescription from a registered medical practitioner.

### Medical Benefits and Legitimate Applications
- **Anesthesia**: Commonly used in surgeries, especially in rural or emergency settings where faster recovery is needed.
- **Pain Relief**: Effective for chronic pain conditions like complex regional pain syndrome.
- **Mental Health**: Emerging as a therapy for severe depression, with clinics in major cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore offering supervised sessions.

Always prioritize FDA-equivalent approvals from the Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO) for safety.
